Nico André Schmid

I am a third year Ph.D. student in the program of Business Economics at Ghent University (advisor: Prof. Veronique Limère). Currently, I am also a visiting scholar at the Georgia Institute of Technology (advisor: Prof. Benoit Montreuil).

Research interests

My Ph.D. thesis is on the optimization of assembly systems with respect to balancing and feeding decisions. Within my master thesis, I investigated the complexity of a job-shop scheduling problem. Furthermore, I am interested in disassembly line balancing and production facility layout. I use mathematical programming and related operations research methods to investigate optimal decision making in those industrial application fields.
